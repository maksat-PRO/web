# maksat.PRO LP Product Ladder

Indicative, non-binding commercial terms for discussion.

## 1. DAO Fund Access
- **Ticket:** $10,000–$100,000
- **Structure:** pooled DAO-style access for smaller LPs through a managed community wrapper and standardized onboarding.
- **Setup fee:** 2% of subscribed amount, minimum $250, capped at $2,000.
- **Management fee:** 3% p.a., subject to final documents.
- **Best for:** smaller tickets, community access, learning-by-participation, early deal-flow exposure.

## 2. Custom CVC Program
- **Ticket:** from $2,000,000
- **Structure:** dedicated CVC mandate for a corporate group.
- **Setup fee:** $100,000–$150,000 for launch architecture, legal coordination, operating model, data room, and first pipeline sprint.
- **Management fee:** 3% p.a. of program capital, with a $15,000/month operating minimum.
- **Best for:** corporate innovation, future business scouting, strategic M&A pipeline, sector intelligence.

## 3. Classic VC Fund Access
- **Ticket:** $500,000–$2,000,000
- **Structure:** classic venture fund exposure with institutional documentation and reporting.
- **Setup fee:** 1% of commitment, minimum $5,000, capped at $20,000.
- **Management fee:** 2% p.a. during the investment period, with step-down mechanics defined in final documents.
- **Best for:** classic LP participation, diversified venture exposure, quarterly reporting, co-investment optionality.

## 4. Dedicated Mono-LP Fund
- **Ticket:** from $10,000,000
- **Structure:** dedicated mono-LP mandate with custom thesis, reporting, and governance package.
- **Setup fee:** from $200,000 for vehicle formation, fund documents, onboarding, operating model, and launch coordination.
- **Management fee:** 2% p.a., with a $200,000/year minimum management fee.
- **Best for:** full mandate control, custom geography/thesis, priority allocation, dedicated reporting.
